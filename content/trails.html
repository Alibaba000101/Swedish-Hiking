The Classic Long-Distance Trails

  • Kungsleden (The King's Trail): 440km through Swedish Lapland, from Abisko to Hemavan. Best: July-September
  • Sormlandsleden: 1000km trail system south of Stockholm, perfect for weekend trips
  • Skaneleden: 1000km of trails across southern Sweden's varied landscapes

Mountain Hiking in Lapland

Swedish Lapland offers spectacular mountain hiking with midnight sun and pristine wilderness:

  • Abisko National Park: Gateway to Lapland hiking, featuring the midnight sun viewpoint
  • Sarek National Park: Wild and remote, for experienced hikers only
  • Kebnekaise: Sweden's highest peak (2,097m), accessible via STF mountain hut

Forest and Lake Hiking

Central Sweden's vast forests and thousands of lakes offer unique hiking experiences:

  • Tiveden National Park: Ancient forest with dramatic boulder landscapes
  • Dalarna region: Rolling hills, traditional villages, and cultural heritage
  • Varmland: Dense forests, pristine lakes, and moose spotting opportunities

Coastal and Archipelago Hiking

Sweden's extensive coastline offers unique hiking experiences:

  • West Coast Trail (Bohusleden): 370km along Sweden's rugged west coast
  • Stockholm Archipelago: Island hopping with ferry connections
  • Gotland: Medieval history combined with coastal landscapes

Most trails are well-marked with orange paint blazes and offer excellent hut systems for multi-day trips.
