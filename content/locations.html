Sweden's Best Hiking Locations

Discover Sweden's most spectacular hiking destinations, from the Arctic wilderness of Lapland to the gentle rolling hills of southern Sweden.

Northern Sweden (Lappland)

Abisko National Park

  • Gateway to the Arctic, starting point of Kungsleden
  • Midnight sun from May to July
  • Excellent for northern lights viewing in winter
  • Well-developed infrastructure with STF mountain station

Sarek National Park

  • Sweden's most remote and challenging wilderness
  • No marked trails or huts - expert navigation required
  • Dramatic mountain peaks and glacial valleys
  • Home to Europe's last wild reindeer herds

Central Sweden (Svealand)

Dalarna Province

  • Traditional Swedish culture and landscapes
  • Rolling forested hills and pristine lakes
  • Excellent for family hiking and cultural experiences
  • Traditional red wooden houses and maypoles

Sormland

  • Extensive trail network near Stockholm
  • Perfect for weekend trips and day hikes
  • Mix of forest, lakes, and cultural heritage sites
  • Accessible by public transportation

Southern Sweden (Gotaland)

Skane Province

  • Gentle landscapes with deciduous forests
  • Coastal trails along dramatic cliffs
  • Rich agricultural landscapes and historic sites
  • Mild climate, hikeable year-round

Smaland Highlands

  • Dense forests, clear lakes, and granite outcrops
  • Birthplace of many Swedish traditions
  • Excellent for mushroom and berry picking
  • Glass kingdom region with artisan workshops

West Coast

Bohuslan

  • Rugged granite coastline and archipelago
  • Unique "skerry" landscape of smooth rock islands
  • Charming fishing villages and seafood culture
  • Rock climbing opportunities on world-class granite

Island Destinations

Gotland

  • Medieval Visby UNESCO World Heritage site
  • Limestone cliffs and unique flora
  • Rich archaeological sites and rune stones
  • Distinctive local culture and traditions

Each region offers unique landscapes and cultural experiences, making Sweden a diverse hiking destination.
